Learning Plan

My goal is to gain an entry level position in the tech industry as a web developer. Long term I would Like to have the confidence and know how to create, manage, and launch a web-based software. Outside of the technical skills I would like to develop my understanding of workflow methodologies and improve my written communication including typing skills.

My strengths are planning and teamwork. My weaknesses are written communication, especially wordy descriptive text. I also struggle for motivation if I feel there is no clear purpose to a task. More detail about my strengths and weaknesses are in my blog “Identity, Values and Strengths Exploration”.

I commit to managing my workload by setting goals each day and keeping to the suggested timeboxes, if I go over the timeboxes I will make that time up where I can.

I commit to communicating as needed with fellow learners and facilitators to get the most out of this experience.

I expect the facilitation team to provide me with constructive feedback and speed up my learning by directing me to useful learning content I need develop my skills and meet my objectives.
